FlatSix Modular Firmaware Uploader

This is a firmware uploaded for arduino-based modules such as Slight Of Hand. It works in Chrome on a Mac or PC.  

  1. Make sure to unplug your module from the eurorack power cable
  2. Plug in your USB cable to the mini usb port on the Slight Of Hand
  3.  The chip next to the USB port will have an "O" or an "N" on it - Click the matching button
  4.  Select the USB option
  5.  Wait until the uploaded finishes 

Very special thanks to: https://github.com/dbuezas/arduino-web-uploader for the updater code.
