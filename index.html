FlatSix Modular Firmware Uploader

Slight Of Hand Firmware V1.5.1 - Released 03-21-2024 

This is a firmware uploader for Arduino-based modules such as Slight Of Hand. It only works in Chrome on a Mac or PC. You will need a USB-A to USB Mini cable. If you don't have one laying around, you can find many inexpensive ones on Amazon   

Read through all the steps below before you update the firmware.

  1. Make sure to unplug your module from the Eurorack power cable first!
  2. Plug in your USB cable to the mini USB port on the Slight Of Hand
  3. The chip next to the USB port will have an "O" or an "N" on it - Click the matching button
  4. If you don't have an N or O on the chip, try "O" first, then "N". Guessing won't hurt anything.
  5. Select the USB serial option from the prompt that pops up.
  6. Wait until the uploaded finishes
  7. You're done. Unplug from the USB cable, plug in the power, and go make some music.
